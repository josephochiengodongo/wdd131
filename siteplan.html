Our team of certified guides is passionate about river stewardship and guest safety. We offer guided rafting trips for all experience levels, from gentle family floats to challenging white-water runs.

History

Founded in 2005 by a group of local river guides, White Water Rafting Company began as a small weekend operation. Over the years we've grown into a full-service outfitter while keeping a strong commitment to conservation, community partnerships, and outdoor education.
